Delta Lives

Delta Lives opens an online meeting space, for the delta communities (starting from the Indian Sundarbans) to know each other and the world. To find global allies with whom they can realise creative potentials, and develop their independent identities. For the first time, they move through the digital waters with oars of art, sound, stories, practices, performances and more. And for the first time, you taste slices of their delta lives from wherever you are in the world.
